Who I am

My name is Jeff Meyer. Originally from St. Louis, I have lived in Connecticut, Massachusetts, and Boulder. Back in Boston, I am looking forward to being an active and valuable part of the burgeoning tech community in Boston.

As recent graduate of the General Assembly Web Development Immersive program, I am now a Full Stack Web Developer. Other than technical skills, this program taught me to learn how to learn. Outside of programming, I love to play golf, snowboard, hang out with my friends and dog and especially travel.
